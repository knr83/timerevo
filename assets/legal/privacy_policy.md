# Privacy Policy — Timerevo

**Last updated:** 2026-02-13

## 1. Overview

Timerevo is an offline desktop application for employee time tracking. All data is stored locally on the device where the application is installed.

**No cloud. No telemetry. No external servers.** The publisher does not operate any backend for Timerevo and does not receive, collect, transmit, or store your data on external servers.

## 2. Roles and Responsibility (GDPR)

Timerevo is typically used by an organization (e.g., an employer) to track employee working time.

- The **organization operating Timerevo** is generally the **data controller** under GDPR because it determines the purposes and means of processing.
- The **publisher of Timerevo** does **not** access or process personal data from the application unless the organization explicitly exports data and shares it for support.

The organization is responsible for:
- employee notices / transparency information,
- retention rules,
- access control and device security,
- lawful basis for processing in the employment context.

## 3. Data We Process (Local Only)

### 3.1 Employee data
Timerevo may store the following fields (depending on what the organization enters):
- First name, last name
- Employee code
- Hire date
- Role (e.g., employee/manager)
- Department, job title
- Email, phone
- Employment type, weekly hours
- Internal comments (free text)

### 3.2 Administrator / user data
- Username
- Credentials (PIN/password) stored in a **secure, encrypted form**
- Audit metadata (e.g., created/updated by)

### 3.3 Time tracking data (work sessions)
- Clock-in / clock-out timestamps
- Employee identifier
- Session duration (derived)
- Notes (optional)
- Audit metadata (if enabled by the app/version)

## 4. Credentials and Security

- PINs and passwords are stored securely and never in plain text.
- Access to the database is controlled by the device’s file system.
- No method can guarantee absolute security. The organization operating Timerevo is responsible for securing the device (accounts, disk encryption, backups, physical access).

## 5. Where Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally in a SQLite database on your device.

Timerevo does not send data over the internet.

## 6. Data Sharing

Timerevo does not share, sell, or transmit your data to third parties.

If the organization chooses to export data (e.g., CSV) or to share files for support, that export/sharing is initiated and controlled by the organization.

## 7. Exports, Backups, and Support

### 7.1 Exports
The application may allow exporting data (e.g., to CSV). Exported files are created on the local device in a location selected by the user/organization.

### 7.2 Backups
If the organization creates backups (manual copies, system backups, or other backup tools), those copies are outside the application’s control.

### 7.3 Support requests
If you contact the publisher for support:
- Avoid sending personal data unless necessary.
- Prefer anonymized or minimal datasets.
- Any data you choose to share for support is shared by the organization voluntarily and under its responsibility.

## 8. Data Deletion

Uninstalling does not always guarantee data deletion (e.g., backups, exports). **For full data removal, contact your administrator.** Data may still remain in backups — delete those separately if needed.

## 9. Third-Party Services

Timerevo does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect product changes or legal requirements. The “Last updated” date at the top indicates the current version.

## 11. Contact

For questions about this policy, please use the **publisher contact details shown in the Microsoft Store listing / application metadata**.

If you use Timerevo as an organization (e.g., an employer) and need GDPR-related assistance, contact your internal responsible person (e.g., management or data protection contact). The publisher does not receive or access your local data unless you explicitly share it for support.
